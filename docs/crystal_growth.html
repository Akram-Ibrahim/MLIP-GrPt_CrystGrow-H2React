Simulation details

Color key: Gray atoms = graphene; colored clusters = Pt atoms (based on the final distinct cluster each Pt atom belongs to).

Time step: 2 fs; each frame in the videos corresponds to 2 ps progression; total simulation ≈ 400 ps.

Process: Physical vapor deposition (PVD) of Pt on graphene at 300 K (room temperature). Deposition is sequential from 0.25 to 2.50 ML (1.00 ML covers 100% of the substrate area).

Substrate size: graphene cell ≈ 15 × 14.8 nm².
